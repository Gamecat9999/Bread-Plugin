# 🥖 BreadPlugin

**BreadPlugin** is a lightweight Minecraft plugin that rewards players with bread when they kill another player—and when they respawn after death. It's a simple mechanic with mythic potential, perfect for PvP servers, tournaments, or lore-driven worlds where carbs are currency.

---

## ⚙️ Features

- 🗡️ **Killer Reward**: When a player kills another player, they instantly receive 5 bread.
- 💀 **Respawn Reward**: When a player dies, they receive 5 bread upon respawning.
- 🍞 **Modular Design**: Easy to extend with lore overlays, particle effects, or config options.
